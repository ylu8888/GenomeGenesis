About Us

Genome Genesis is a tool for exploring statistical data related to cancer. It is dedicated to providing up to date information and analytics regarding the relationships between cancer and age, gender, lifestyles, drinking habits, etc. This website was created by the WebGenomics VIP Team at Stony Brook University in 2023. The team is based in New York and consists of Aadith Samala, Yang Lu, and Brian Park. We hope you enjoy and learn a lot about these interesting cancer data statistics :)
