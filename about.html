About Genome Genesis

Genome Genesis is a tool for exploring cancer data and other medical information. It utilizes data retrieved from several APIs such as the GDC, Sanger, and SerpAPI. Users may visualize cancer genomics data utilizing charts, graphs, violin plots, and heat maps. They can also explore medical science research articles that are new and up-to-date from Google Scholar within the SerpAPI. Genome Genesis was created in Spring 2024 by Aadith Samala, Brian Park, Yang Lu, and Brandon Wong. It is in collaboration with the WebGenomics VIP team at Stony Brook University
